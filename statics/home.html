Busuanzi Alternative for Your Website

Powered by Vercel and Redis. Vercount is a simple, fast, and secure website counter that you can use to track your website's visitor count. It's a great alternative to Busuanzi, and it's completely free to use.

Why Choose Vercount?

Vercount is designed to provide you with accurate, real-time visitor count data for your website. Here are some of the key features that make Vercount the best choice for your website analytics needs.

Real-time Data

Get real-time visitor count data for your website. No more waiting for data to refresh.

Fast Performance

Vercount is designed to be fast and efficient, ensuring that your website's performance is never compromised.

Secure Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties. You can trust Vercount to keep your data safe.

Get Started

How to Use Vercount

Using Vercount is as simple as inserting a script tag and using a span with the corresponding id.

  1. Insert the following script tag into your HTML file: 
    <script defer src="https://vercount.one/js"></script>
  2. Use the following span tags to display the desired data: 
    Total Page View <span id="busuanzi_page_pv"></span>
Total Visitors <span id="busuanzi_page_uv"></span>
Total Visits <span id="busuanzi_site_pv"></span>
Site Total Visitors <span id="busuanzi_site_uv"></span>
  3. Save your changes and refresh your website to see the visitor count.
